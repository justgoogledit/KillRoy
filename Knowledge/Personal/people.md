# People

Coworkers, collaborators, regular contacts. Helps Claude know who's who when you mention names.

## Format

For each person, capture: name, role/team, what they care about, how you usually interact, anything else that helps Claude reason about them.

## Examples

- **Name** — role/team. Cares about X. You usually interact via Y. *(replace with real people)*

## Add more as they come up

When `session-recap` notices a new name, it should suggest adding an entry here.
